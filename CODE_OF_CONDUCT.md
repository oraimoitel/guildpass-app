# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct <!-- IC: 212 -->

## Our Pledge <!-- IC: 213 -->

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the **GuildPass** community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, colour, religion, or sexual identity and orientation. <!-- IC: 214 -->

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community. <!-- IC: 215 -->

## Our Standards <!-- IC: 216 -->

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment: <!-- IC: 217 -->

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people <!-- IC: 218 -->
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences <!-- IC: 219 -->
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback <!-- IC: 220 -->
- Accepting responsibility and apologising to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience <!-- IC: 221 -->
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community <!-- IC: 222 -->

Examples of unacceptable behaviour: <!-- IC: 223 -->

- The use of sexualised language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind <!-- IC: 224 -->
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks <!-- IC: 225 -->
- Public or private harassment <!-- IC: 226 -->
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without their explicit permission <!-- IC: 227 -->
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting <!-- IC: 228 -->

## Enforcement Responsibilities <!-- IC: 229 -->

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behaviour and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behaviour that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. <!-- IC: 230 -->

## Scope <!-- IC: 231 -->

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. <!-- IC: 232 -->

## Enforcement <!-- IC: 233 -->

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at **maintainers@guildpass.xyz**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. <!-- IC: 234 -->

## Enforcement Guidelines <!-- IC: 235 -->

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct: <!-- IC: 236 -->

### 1. Correction <!-- IC: 237 -->
**Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behaviour deemed unprofessional.   <!-- IC: 238 -->
**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders. <!-- IC: 239 -->

### 2. Warning <!-- IC: 240 -->
**Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.   <!-- IC: 241 -->
**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behaviour.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact**: A serious violation of community standards.  
**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards.  
**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
